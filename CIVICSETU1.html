🧰 Materials Required for Basic First Aid

  • Sterile gauze pads and bandages
  • Adhesive tape and plasters
  • Antiseptic wipes and cream

  • CPR face shield
  • Gloves (disposable, non-latex)
  • Digital thermometer
  • Burn gel or cooling dressing
  • Safety pins and triangular bandage
  • Emergency contact card and notepad

AdhikarSetu

This module empowers citizens to exercise their rights effectively. Learn about the Right to Information (RTI) and the process of filing Petitions against Government actions.

📄 Right to Information (RTI)

What? RTI allows any citizen to request information from a government office or department.

Why? It ensures transparency and accountability in governance.

How? File an RTI application online via RTI Online or submit a written application to the concerned public authority with a ₹10 fee.

📝 Petition Against Government

What? A petition is a formal written request to a government authority seeking justice or policy change.

Why? Citizens use petitions to raise collective concerns, challenge unjust laws, and influence governance.

How?

  • Draft a clear statement of grievance.
  • Gather supporting documents and public signatures.
  • Submit it to the concerned government department or online portals like Centralized Public Grievance Redress And Monitoring System or state grievance portals.
